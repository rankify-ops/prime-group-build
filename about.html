About Prime Group

Built on reliability and driven by quality — we're a registered building company with 15+ years of hands-on experience delivering quality construction across Victoria.

Our Story

15+ Years Building Trust Across Victoria

Prime Group Building was founded on a simple belief: every project deserves the same level of care, craftsmanship, and reliability — whether it's a granny flat in the suburbs or a full commercial office fit-out in the city.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, we've built a reputation for delivering quality results on time and on budget. Our team brings deep expertise across residential, commercial, and NDIS building solutions.

Based in Victoria with expansion into Queensland coming soon, we're proud to serve homeowners, businesses, and NDIS participants across Greater Melbourne and beyond.

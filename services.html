Residential

Granny Flats & Tiny Homes

Add value and extra living space to your property with a custom-built granny flat or tiny home. Perfect for families, rental income, or accommodating elderly parents close to home.

We handle everything from design through to construction and handover. Permits are usually organised by the client, but we can help manage the process and recommend trusted professionals.

  • Custom designs to suit your block and budget
  • Only a building permit needed for 60m² and under
  • Self-contained with kitchen, bathroom & laundry
  • Energy-efficient builds with quality finishes

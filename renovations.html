FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about home renovations in Melbourne.

How much does a bathroom renovation cost in Melbourne?

Bathroom renovation costs vary based on the size of the room, scope of work, and quality of finishes selected. We provide a detailed fixed-price quote after an in-home consultation so you know the full cost upfront. Contact us for a free assessment and obligation-free quote.

How long does a kitchen renovation take?

A typical kitchen renovation takes between 4 to 8 weeks depending on the scope of the project. If structural changes are involved, the timeline may extend. We provide a detailed schedule before work begins and keep you updated on progress throughout the build.

Do I need a permit for my renovation?

Building permits are required for structural work, plumbing changes, and electrical modifications. Cosmetic updates like painting or replacing fixtures typically don't require a permit. We advise you on permit requirements during the quoting stage and manage all applications on your behalf.

Can I live in my home during the renovation?

In most cases, yes. We plan the work to minimise disruption to your household, staging the project so you always have access to essential facilities. For major whole-home renovations, we'll discuss the best approach during the planning phase.

Do you handle heritage or period home renovations?

Yes, we have experience working with heritage and period homes across Melbourne. We understand the requirements for heritage overlays and work sensitively to preserve the character of your home while modernising key areas. All heritage-related permits are managed by our team.

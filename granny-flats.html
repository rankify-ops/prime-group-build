FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about building a granny flat or tiny home in Melbourne.

How much does a granny flat cost to build in Melbourne?

The cost of a granny flat varies depending on size, design complexity, site conditions, and finishes selected. We provide detailed fixed-price quotes after a free site assessment so you know exactly what to expect with no hidden costs. Contact us for an obligation-free consultation and quote tailored to your property.

Do I need a council permit for a granny flat in Victoria?

Yes, a granny flat (also known as a dependent person's unit or secondary dwelling) typically requires a planning permit and building permit in Victoria. We manage the entire permit process on your behalf, including preparing and submitting all required documentation to your local council.

How long does it take to build a granny flat?

Construction typically takes between 12 to 20 weeks depending on the size and complexity of the build. The permit process can add 8 to 12 weeks before construction begins. We provide a clear timeline during the quoting stage and keep you updated on progress throughout.

Can I rent out my granny flat?

Rental options depend on your local council's planning scheme and any conditions attached to your permit. Many granny flats in Victoria are approved for use by a dependent person (family member), though some councils allow broader usage. We can advise on the best approach for your situation during the design phase.

What size granny flat can I build on my property?

The maximum size depends on your property's zoning, lot size, and council regulations. Most granny flats range from 40 to 60 square metres. During our free site visit, we assess your property and advise on the maximum allowable footprint and the best layout for your needs.

Are you a registered builder?

Yes, Prime Group Building is a fully registered builder in Victoria with over 15 years of experience. All our work is covered by the required domestic building insurance and builder's warranty, giving you complete peace of mind throughout your project.

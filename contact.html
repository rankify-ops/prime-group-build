FAQ

Common questions

How do I get a quote?

Simply fill out the form above, give us a call on 0468 866 046, or send an email to admin@primegroupbuild.com. We'll arrange a free site visit and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.

What areas do you service?

We service all of Greater Melbourne and surrounding Victorian regions. We're also expanding into Queensland soon.

Are you a registered builder?

Yes, we're a fully registered building company with the Victorian Building Authority. We also hold comprehensive public liability and professional indemnity insurance.

Do you handle NDIS modifications?

Yes, we're a registered NDIS supplier. We specialise in home modifications that improve accessibility and independence, fully compliant with NDIS building standards.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency response and critical maintenance support. Call us anytime on 0468 866 046 for urgent assistance.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope — a bathroom renovation might take 2–3 weeks, while a granny flat could be 8–12 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront during the quote process.

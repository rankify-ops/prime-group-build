Registered Builder • Montmorency

Building & Renovation Specialists for Montmorency Homes

Montmorency is known for its peaceful streets lined with 1960s and 70s brick veneer homes, set against a backdrop of native bush leading toward Eltham and the Nillumbik foothills. Prime Group Building has spent over 15 years helping local families modernise these established homes — opening up dark floor plans, upgrading kitchens and bathrooms to current standards, and adding secondary dwellings to generous backyard blocks near Montmorency Village and the Were Street shops.

Many Montmorency properties sit on sloping, treed lots that require careful site management during construction. Our team is experienced with the specific challenges of building in bushy suburban settings, from vegetation overlays to stormwater considerations on hilly terrain. Whether you want a self-contained granny flat for ageing parents, an NDIS-compliant bathroom modification, or a full home renovation, we handle the build from permits through to handover. Contact us for an obligation-free quote.

  • Over 15 years as a registered domestic and commercial builder
  • Self-contained granny flats and backyard dwellings
  • Full home renovations — kitchens, bathrooms, open-plan conversions
  • Shopfront and business fit-outs along Were Street and surrounds
  • Accessible home modifications for NDIS participants (registered supplier)
  • After-hours and emergency building repairs, available 24/7
  • End-to-end construction management for larger builds

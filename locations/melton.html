Helping Melton Families Get More from Their Homes

Melton is one of Melbourne's fastest-growing areas, with new housing estates spreading across the western corridor and young families putting down roots. While many homes here are relatively new, that doesn't mean there's nothing to build — quite the opposite. Growing families need extra bedrooms, outdoor entertaining areas, upgraded kitchens, and self-contained granny flats for extended family members. Prime Group Building specialises in exactly these kinds of practical, value-adding projects, with 15+ years of experience working efficiently on modern estate blocks.

We also fit out commercial and retail spaces around Melton Town Centre as the local economy keeps pace with population growth, and we're a registered NDIS supplier delivering accessibility modifications for residents of all abilities. Our pricing is transparent, our timelines are realistic, and we stand behind our work. Get your free Melton building quote today.

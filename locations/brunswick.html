Building & Renovating in Brunswick's Inner North

Brunswick is a suburb of contrasts — ornate Victorian terraces with cast-iron lacework sit next to sleek modern townhouses, while Sydney Road buzzes with independent cafes, galleries, and multicultural retail. Building here means working with narrow lots, shared walls, heritage-protected facades, and neighbours who are close by. Prime Group Building has spent over 15 years mastering the specific challenges of inner-north construction, from underpinning old bluestone foundations to designing rear extensions that flood narrow terrace floor plans with natural light.

Our Brunswick work spans everything from full gut-and-renovate projects on period terraces to cafe and retail fit-outs along the Sydney Road strip. We also build compact granny flats and studio dwellings on smaller lots, and carry out NDIS modifications in older homes where accessibility was never part of the original design. As a registered builder and NDIS registered supplier, we manage permits, party wall agreements, and heritage approvals so you don't have to. Talk to us about your Brunswick project today.

  • Compact granny flats & studio builds on narrow Brunswick lots
  • Victorian terrace renovations — kitchens, bathrooms & rear extensions

  • NDIS accessibility modifications in heritage homes (registered supplier)

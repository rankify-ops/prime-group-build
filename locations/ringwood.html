Registered Builder • Ringwood

Ringwood's Go-To Builder for Renovations & New Builds

Ringwood sits at the heart of Melbourne's eastern redevelopment boom. Around the Eastland precinct and Ringwood Lake, older fibro and weatherboard homes are being transformed into modern family residences, while new townhouse developments continue to reshape the streetscape. Prime Group Building works across this full spectrum — renovating established homes that still have great bones, constructing secondary dwellings on subdivided lots, and delivering commercial fit-outs for the growing retail and office corridor along Maroondah Highway.

Our 15+ years as a registered builder means we know how to manage the council and planning processes in the Maroondah municipality efficiently. We are also an NDIS registered supplier, providing compliant accessibility modifications for participants across Ringwood and neighbouring suburbs. If you need a builder who understands the pace and expectations of this rapidly evolving area, get in touch for a free quote.

  • Registered domestic and commercial builder, 15+ years in the trade
  • Compact granny flats and secondary dwellings for subdivided blocks
  • Kitchen, bathroom, and whole-home renovations for older Ringwood properties
  • Retail and office fit-outs serving the Eastland and Maroondah Highway corridor
  • NDIS accessibility upgrades — registered supplier with compliant builds
  • Round-the-clock emergency repairs and urgent maintenance callouts
  • Project management from planning permits to practical completion

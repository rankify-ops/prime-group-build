Emergency Services

Types of Emergencies We Handle

Our team is equipped to respond to a wide range of building emergencies quickly and professionally.

Storm & Weather Damage

Roof damage, fallen trees, broken windows, and water ingress from severe weather events. We secure your property and begin repairs immediately.

Water Damage & Leaks

Burst pipes, roof leaks, flooding, and water damage containment. Fast response to minimise damage and begin the drying and repair process.

Structural Emergencies

Cracked foundations, wall damage, ceiling collapses, and structural failures. We assess the situation, make the area safe, and carry out urgent repairs.

Fire & Smoke Damage

Post-fire building assessment, securing the property, and beginning the restoration process. We work with insurance companies to document all damage.

Security & Make-Safe

Emergency board-ups, broken door and window repairs, lock changes, and temporary security measures to protect your property after a break-in or damage event.

Critical Maintenance

Urgent maintenance issues that can't wait — failed hot water systems, electrical hazards, gas leaks, and other critical building services failures.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our emergency building services.

Are you really available 24/7?

Yes. Our emergency line is monitored around the clock, 365 days a year. Whether it's 2am on a Sunday or Christmas Day, call us on 0404 972 047 and we'll respond to your emergency.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

Response times depend on your location and the nature of the emergency. We prioritise all emergency calls and aim to have a team on-site as quickly as possible. For life-threatening situations, always call 000 first.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes. We document all damage thoroughly with photographs and written reports, provide detailed quotes for repairs, and can liaise directly with your insurance company. We work with all major Australian insurers and understand their requirements.

What should I do in a building emergency?

First, ensure everyone's safety — evacuate if necessary and call 000 for life-threatening situations. Turn off water or electricity at the mains if safe to do so. Then call us on 0404 972 047. Take photos of the damage if it's safe, and avoid entering damaged areas until they've been assessed.

Do you handle both residential and commercial emergencies?

Yes. Our emergency response covers both residential homes and commercial premises across Melbourne. Whether it's a family home, office building, retail space, or warehouse, we have the experience and resources to respond.

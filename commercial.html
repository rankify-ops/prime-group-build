FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about commercial fit-outs in Melbourne.

How long does a commercial fit-out take?

Timelines vary based on the size and complexity of the project. A standard office fit-out typically takes 4 to 10 weeks, while larger or more complex projects may take longer. We provide a detailed project schedule during the quoting phase and keep you updated throughout.

Can you work after hours to avoid disrupting our business?

Absolutely. We regularly schedule commercial work outside of business hours, including evenings and weekends. We can also stage the project in phases so that parts of your premises remain operational while other areas are being fitted out.

Do you handle all the permits and compliance?

Yes, we manage all building permits, council approvals, and ensure your fit-out complies with the Building Code of Australia, fire safety standards, accessibility requirements, and any specific regulations for your industry.

Can you work from an architect's or designer's plans?

Yes, we work with architects, interior designers, and project managers. If you already have plans, we can quote and build from them. If you need design assistance, we can recommend trusted partners or provide guidance based on our experience.

What types of commercial spaces do you fit out?

We handle a wide range of commercial projects including corporate offices, retail stores, restaurants and cafes, medical and dental practices, warehouses, co-working spaces, and mixed-use developments. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
